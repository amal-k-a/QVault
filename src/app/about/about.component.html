About Kristu Jayanti University

Kristu Jayanti University, located in Bengaluru, was established in 1999 and is managed by the Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI). Recognized as a Deemed-to-be University, it offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in arts, science, commerce, management, law, and social sciences. Accredited with an 'A+' grade by NAAC, the university is committed to academic excellence, ethical leadership, and global relevance.

With a vibrant campus, innovative curriculum, and strong research and holistic development focus, it empowers students to thrive in a rapidly evolving world.
